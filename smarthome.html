Technical Implementation

The Smart Home Security and Safety System is built on a foundation of digital logic design principles combined with modern microcontroller technology. At its core, an Arduino Mega processes inputs from various sensors throughout the home.

Our system uses a network of infrared motion detectors, magnetic door/window sensors, smoke detectors, and environmental sensors to monitor the home 24/7. These sensors feed data to our central processing unit which analyzes patterns and triggers appropriate responses.

We implemented custom digital logic circuits to manage alarm states, sensor prioritization, and fail-safe mechanisms. The system features three operational modes: Home, Away, and Night - each with customized security profiles.
